IVY KINYANJUI

I will talk about who i think i am. I am the kind of girl who is quiet in large groups or around people i dont know.
You only see the real me if we are close. I smile and laugh a lot,especially at the most inappropriate times. Im a hopeless romantic
I trip over air,up stairs,and over people's feet.I am the hardest person to offend,but it is all too easy to make me feel horrible
I hate telling people my problems;they don't need to worry about me.
I'm the one who listens to other people's problems. I believe people should not be judged before one takes the time to get to know them
Yet i am guilty of doing that exact thing
I love to think rather than talk.
I'm awkward ,cllumsy,shy,strange but this is me. Take it leave it

SPIRIT ANIMAL

My spirit animal is a butterfly since they are a remider of our own spiritual transformation.
Moreso, i view the butterfly as proof you can go through a great deal of darkness and still become something new.
I love the butterfly since it reminds us that its never too late to transform ourselves
